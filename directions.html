Banana Bread recipe

Steps to Make Banana Bread

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x5 inch loaf pan.
  2. In a large bowl, combine flour, baking soda and salt. In a separate bowl, cream together butter and brown sugar.
  3. Stir in eggs and mashed bananas until well blended. Stir banana mixture into flour mixture; stir just to moisten.
  4. Pour batter into prepared loaf pan.
  5. Bake in preheated oven for 60 to 65 minutes, until a toothpick inserted into center of the loaf comes out clean.
  6. Let bread cool in pan for 10 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack.
